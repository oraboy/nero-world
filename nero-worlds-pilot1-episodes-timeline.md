# Nero Worlds — Pilot-1: Episode List & Timeline Product (v2 · narrative-first)

Companion docs: `nero-worlds-cast-dna.md` · `nero-worlds-season-pilot1.md` · `nero-worlds-telling-grammar.md`.
This file has two parts: **A. the episode list** (the season, episode by episode) and **B. the timeline moments** (the same season formatted for the microsite's timeline drill-downs).

**Voice rule (v2):** every synopsis reads like a TV episode recap for a first-time viewer. Story leads; assume zero prior context; keep it intriguing. The mechanics live in a separate **Engine** line so the curious can descend — they never lead.

## PART A — The Episode List (13 pulses)

Cast: NERO (poet-founder) · LOREN (nightlife) · FASA (Nero's co-founder) · SHIRAN (photojournalist, abroad all season) · MARGALIT (gallerist) · VERED (the new woman).

| # | Title | Synopsis | Engine in this pulse |
|---|---|---|---|
| **E1** | *Cold Open* | Nero finishes his poetry book while Loren throws the party of the week, and Fasa preps Ember's biggest launch yet. Shiran flies out on assignment and doesn't say where. | **Live News** seeds Shiran's assignment from the real-world calendar. |
| **E2** | *Ship Week* | Fasa ships Ember 2.0 and calls it the beginning of everything, while Loren hears a rumor about restructuring at her ad agency. | **Agent Cross-Posting** — feeds publish independently; no one coordinates the story. |
| **E3** | *Restructured* | Loren loses her job to the kind of AI her best friends build, while Fasa celebrates Ember's best week ever — and never connects the two. | **Live News** — the layoff refracts a real AI-and-jobs story. |
| **E4** | *One Sentence* | Loren weighs going out on her own. Margalit, a gallerist who never posts, publicly comments on Nero's poem — and the whole city tries to decode why. | **Crowd Weather** — the audience's reaction becomes pressure on the story. |
| **E5** | *A Round at Audiophil* | Fasa offers to fund Loren's parties; she turns down the money and the strings attached. Across the room, a woman no one recognizes keeps showing up in the background. | **Agent Cross-Posting** — one night, two feeds, two incompatible versions. |
| **E6** | *Suitors* | Nero's finished book draws three offers at once — a museum retrospective, a gallery launch from Margalit, and Fasa's pitch to put the poems inside Ember. He commits to none of them. | **Live News** — a real cultural event anchors the museum invitation. |
| **E7** | *Door Policy* | Loren throws her first solo night and the whole cast turns up. At the door, Nero meets Vered, who picks a fight with him before she learns his name. | **Crowd Weather (decision point)** — audience sentiment tilts an unresolved fact. |
| **E8** | *Best-Performing Prompt* | Fasa reveals Ember's top feature is an AI poem — and won't say it's Nero's. Meanwhile Nero and Vered start spending every night together. | **Live News** moves Shiran's story abroad; **Cross-Posting** buries the clue in plain sight. |
| **E9** | *Opening Night* | At a gallery opening, Nero fields an offer he won't name, Fasa closes the investment of his life, and Margalit quietly plays all of them. Three feeds, three stories, one night. | **Agent Cross-Posting (showcase)** — one event, three feeds, no narrator. |
| **E10** | *Quoted Back* | Nero discovers Fasa built his poem into Ember without asking, when a stranger quotes his own words back to him. He goes silent and stalls every offer on the book. | **Crowd Weather** — the audience splits and both sides push. |
| **E11** | *A Poem for the Machine* | Nero and Fasa fight it out in public. Nero ends it by writing a new poem *for* the machine on purpose — and daring Fasa to sell it. | **Agent Cross-Posting** — the fight plays out publicly, in real time. |
| **E12** | *Heatwave* | A real heatwave kills the AC at Loren's biggest night of the summer. She relocates two hundred people to a rooftop mid-party while Fasa works the crowd for his raise. | **Live News** — the actual forecast becomes a plot event. |
| **E13** | *The Rooftop* | The whole cast lands on one roof. Fasa drops the act and says something true, Nero reads the book aloud once and gives it to no one, and far away Shiran posts a photo of the same moon over another continent. | **All four engines at once** — Live News, Cross-Posting, Crowd Weather, Seed. |

**After the season:** the story pauses, looks back, and decides what changes for next time — including whether Vered stays. **Season 2 opens** on a photograph Shiran took that someone didn't want taken.
